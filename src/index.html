Concentration Card Game

Welcome to the Concentration Card Game!

How to play:

  • 52 cards are layed out face down, in 4 rows, 13 cards in each row,

  • Click on the cards to flip them, if the two cards match they are removed from the board

  • The game ends once you have matched all of the pairs!

  • At any time you can click 'New Game' to re-shuffle the deck and the board.

  • Hit 'New Game'to test your concentration and memory!
